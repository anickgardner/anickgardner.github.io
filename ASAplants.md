## Introduction

### Glossary

**Acetylsalicylic acid or acetyl salicylic acid (ASA).** Also known as 2-acetoxybenzoic acid, acetylsalicylate, *O*-acetylsalicylic acid. This compound is used to reduce pain, fever or inflammation.^1
